Places of Interest in Sri Lanka

Colombo

The vibrant commercial capital blending colonial heritage, shopping, temples, and modern city life.

Negombo

Coastal fishing town famous for lagoons, beaches, and lively fish markets.

Chilaw

Peaceful seaside town known for Munneswaram Temple and coconut plantations.

Wilpattu Safari

Sri Lanka’s largest national park known for leopards, lakes, and untouched wilderness.

Pinnawala

Famous elephant orphanage where herds bathe in the Ma Oya river.

Sigiriya

UNESCO rock fortress featuring frescoes, mirror wall, and panoramic views.

Kandy

Hill capital home to the Temple of the Tooth Relic and scenic lake.

Nuwara Eliya

Tea country known as Little England with cool climate and waterfalls.

Ella

Mountain village with Nine Arch Bridge, hikes, and breathtaking views.

Yala Safari

Wildlife hotspot with the highest leopard density in the world.

Mirissa

Golden beaches famous for whale watching and coastal relaxation.

Galle

Historic Dutch fort city with charming streets and ocean views.
