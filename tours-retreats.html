Tour & Retreat Packages

At JOY Project, we bring 30+ years of hospitality expertise to craft exceptional Sri Lankan experiences.

Our tailor-made tour and retreat packages are designed to meet your unique needs, ensuring an authentic and unforgettable journey through the beauty of Sri Lanka.

Let’s create a memorable adventure!

All-Inclusive Package Benefits

Enjoy a hassle-free travel journey with our all-inclusive package. From your arrival in Sri Lanka to your departure, our local Ground Tour Operator team is dedicated to serving you. We handle accommodations, transportation, meals, activities, and all experiences, ensuring every detail is meticulously managed. Relax and fully enjoy your trip while we take care of everything.

Begin Your Sri Lanka Adventure

Fill out the form on our contact page, and we’ll set up a fun online consultation! As the Group Leader, you’ll share your group’s interests, and we’ll create a personalised itinerary just for you. From start to finish, your experience will be stress-free, and unforgettable.

WHO WE SERVE

Our tours and retreats attract a diverse range of clients, each seeking unique experiences in Sri Lanka’s stunning landscapes

Wellness Groups

Sri Lanka is a top destination for wellness, offering Ayurveda, yoga, meditation, and more. We tailor retreats to meet your group's needs, ensuring a peaceful and rejuvenating experience.

For Wellness Instructors: Lead your own retreat or collaborate with local experts to enhance your program

For Wellness Enthusiasts: Whether a student or practitioner, we connect you with expert facilitators for transformative experiences.

Friends & Family

Create unforgettable memories with your loved ones in Sri Lanka. From adventurous excursions to relaxing beachside escapes, we ensure a seamless, enjoyable experience for all.

Spiritual Seekers & Pilgrims

Embark on a spiritual journey through Sri Lanka’s sacred sites, from Anuradhapura to the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic. These tours offer enlightenment and deeper connection to the island’s spiritual heritage.

History Enthusiasts & Culture Lovers

Explore Sri Lanka’s rich history and culture, from ancient ruins to vibrant festivals. Perfect for curious travellers and scholars, our tours bring the island’s past to life with authenticity and depth. *Student & Education packages available!

Corporate Teams

Elevate your corporate retreats with immersive experiences in Sri Lanka. We design programs that blend strategic goals with dynamic tours, team-building activities, and wellness retreats to inspire and energize your team.

Couples: Romantic Getaways, Weddings, & Honeymoons

Celebrate love in Sri Lanka’s most romantic settings. From secluded beaches to luxurious resorts, we create unforgettable experiences for honeymoons, weddings, and romantic getaways.
