The Radiant Pearl Adventure

Introduction

Experience Sri Lanka’s most iconic highlights on this immersive journey from Negombo to Colombo. Meet elephants in Pinnawala, conquer Sigiriya, explore Kandy’s sacred heritage, travel through tea country, trek Horton Plains, discover Ella’s scenic bridges, enjoy Yala safari adventures, relax on Mirissa’s golden shores, and explore Galle Fort before ending in vibrant Colombo.

8 Nights / 9 Days

Day 1: Negombo – The Gateway to the Island

  • The Experience: Spend the morning at Negombo Beach, watching the "Oruwa" (traditional outrigger canoes) return with their catch. Your City Tour will lead you through the Dutch Canal, a relic of colonial engineering, and the St. Mary’s Church, famous for its ceiling paintings.
  • Culture & Heritage: Visit the bustling Negombo Fish Market (Lellama) to see the 18th-century "Madu" drying process.
  • Nature: The serene lagoon ecosystem provides a backdrop of mangroves and local birdlife.
  • Value Addition: We recommend a brief stop at a local bakery to try "Roast Paan" (wood-fired bread)—a true staple of the Negombo fishing community.

Stay overnight in Negambo

Day 2 – Pinnawala & Minneriya

Elephant river bath and Minneriya safari witnessing wild elephant gatherings.

Day 3 – Sigiriya & Kandy

Sigiriya climb, Dambulla Temple, Spice Garden massage, Kandy dance show & Temple visit.

Day 4 – Nuwara Eliya

Peradeniya Gardens, Tea Factory tour, Ramboda Falls, Little England exploration.

Day 5 – Horton Plains & Ella

World’s End trek, Hakgala Garden, Ella Gap sunset.

Day 6 – Tissamaharama

Nine Arch Bridge, Ravana Falls, Kataragama Puja ceremony.

Day 7 – Yala & Mirissa

Leopard safari, stilt fishermen stop, Mirissa beach relaxation.

Day 8 – Galle & Colombo

Galle Fort walk, Kosgoda turtles, Colombo city highlights.

Day 9 – Departure

Souvenir shopping and airport transfer with Ayubowan farewell.
