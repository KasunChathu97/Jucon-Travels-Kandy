The Radiant Pearl Adventure

8 Nights / 9 Days

Introduction

Experience the quintessential charm of Sri Lanka on this 9-day journey starting from Negombo’s golden shores. Encounter elephants at Pinnawala and Minneriya, explore Sigiriya Rock Fortress and Dambulla Cave Temple, and immerse yourself in Kandy’s spiritual heritage. Travel through tea estates of Nuwara Eliya, trek Horton Plains, discover Ella’s Nine Arch Bridge and Ravana Falls, experience Kataragama’s sacred rituals, enjoy a Yala safari, relax in Mirissa, explore Galle Fort, and end with a vibrant Colombo city finale.

Day 1 – Negombo: Gateway to the Island

Negombo Beach, Dutch Canal, St. Mary’s Church, Fish Market visit, lagoon nature, and local Roast Paan tasting.

Day 2 – Pinnawala & Minneriya Safari

Elephant orphanage river bath and Minneriya safari to witness “The Gathering” of wild elephants.

Day 3 – Sigiriya & Kandy

Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Dambulla Temple, Spice Garden Ayurvedic massage, Kandy dance show & Tooth Relic Temple.

Day 4 – Kandy to Nuwara Eliya

Peradeniya Gardens, Labukale Tea Factory, Ramboda Falls, and Little England city tour with tea tasting.

Day 5 – Horton Plains & Ella

World’s End trek, Hakgala Gardens, Ella Gap sunset viewpoint.

Day 6 – Ella to Tissamaharama

Nine Arch Bridge, Mini World’s End, Ravana Falls, Kataragama evening Puja ceremony.

Day 7 – Yala Safari & Mirissa

Sunrise Yala safari, leopard tracking, stilt fishermen stop, Mirissa beach relaxation.

Day 8 – Galle & Colombo

Galle Fort exploration, Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, Colombo city tour and sunset rampart walk.

Day 9 – Departure

Shopping at Laksala & Barefoot, airport transfer with traditional Ayubowan farewell.
